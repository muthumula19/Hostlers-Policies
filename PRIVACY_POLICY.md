# Privacy Policy for Hostler

**Last Updated: December 3, 2025**

**Effective Date: December 3, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Hostler ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("Hostler" or the "App"). Hostler is a hostel management platform that connects hostel owners with guests, facilitating room bookings, rent payments, and accommodation management.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the App.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when registering for an account or using the App:

**Account Information:**
- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Password (stored securely in encrypted format)
- User role (Guest, Owner, or Admin)

**Profile Information (for Guests):**
- Age
- Gender
- Permanent address
- Company name and employment status
- Profile photo
- Emergency contact details (name, relationship, phone number)

**Identity Verification Documents:**
- Address proof documents (Aadhaar Card, Passport, Driving License, Voter ID, PAN Card, Utility Bill, Bank Statement)
- Document images uploaded for verification purposes

**Hostel Owner Information:**
- Hostel details (name, address, amenities)
- Room configurations and pricing
- Payment account details (UPI IDs, bank account information)

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

**Device Information:**
- Device type and model
- Operating system and version
- Unique device identifiers (Device ID)
- Device name

**Usage Information:**
- Login timestamps
- App usage patterns
- Feature interactions

### 2.3 Location Information

We collect location data when you:
- Register a hostel (to set hostel location on map)
- Search for hostels nearby
- Use map features within the App

Location data is collected only when you grant permission and is used solely for the features described above.

### 2.4 Biometric Data

If you choose to enable biometric authentication (fingerprint or Face ID), we:
- Use your device's biometric authentication system
- Store a device-specific token to enable quick login
- Do NOT store or transmit your actual biometric data (fingerprints, face scans)

### 2.5 Payment Information

**Payment Records:**
- Payment amounts and dates
- Payment method types (UPI, Cash, Bank Transfer, Card)
- Transaction IDs
- Payment status and history
- Rent records and billing information

**Note:** We do not store complete credit/debit card numbers. Payment processing is handled through secure third-party payment providers.

### 2.6 Communication Data

- Support ticket content and attachments
- Comments between guests and hostel owners
- Notification preferences

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Data Storage

- Your data is stored on secure cloud servers (Microsoft Azure)
- Data is stored in encrypted format
- Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device using encrypted storage

### 5.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- Secure token-based authentication (JWT)
- Encrypted storage for sensitive data (passwords, tokens)
- Rate limiting to prevent brute force attacks
- Regular security audits and updates
- Automatic session timeout for inactive users

### 5.3 Data Retention

- Active account data is retained while your account is active
- Payment records are retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance
- Deleted accounts are permanently removed within 30 days
- Backup data is purged within 90 days of account deletion
